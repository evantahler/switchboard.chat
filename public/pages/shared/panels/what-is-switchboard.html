What is swithboard.chat?

Switchboard.chat is a hosted web application which enables teams to communicate with thier remote employees via SMS. Using a shared, hosted SMS number allows:

  • Many people to share a number without access to a physical phone
  • A mobile-friendly website you can access whenever you need
  • A shared log of all communications both in and out of the shared number
  • Multiple accounts so you can add and remove team members as needed
  • Customizable notifications of unread messages
  • The separation of work and home via a new number
