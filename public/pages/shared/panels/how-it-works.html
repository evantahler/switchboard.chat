How does switchboard.chat work?

When you sign up for switchboard.chat, you will be creating your team and the first memeber of that team.. you.

Each team has 1 phone number used for SMS communication. You can have unlimited members of your team, and unlimited people in your address book whom you are communicating with.

Registering a team costs $5.00/month. This covers the cost of your phone number and the fist 100 SMS messages. Every additional message after that costs 1 cent.

When you create your team, you will be randomly assigned a phone number within the area code you choose. This service only works within the United States at this time.
