How does switchboard.chat work?

When you sign up for switchboard.chat, you will be creating your team and the first memeber of that team.. you.

Each team has 1 phone number used for SMS communication. You can have unlimited members of your team, and unlimited people in your address book whom you are communicating with.

Registering a team costs ${{ $root.billing.rates.pricePerMonth / 100 }}/month. This covers the cost of your phone number and the fist {{ $root.billing.rates.includedMessagesPerMonth }} SMS messages. Every additional message after that costs ${{ $root.billing.rates.pricePerMessage / 100 }}. You will be billed the montly fee at the start of every month or when you first sign up, and the fee for any additional messages at the end of the month.
